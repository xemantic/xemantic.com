Key Market Insights

Industry Trends

  • Technology sector offers the highest average salaries
  • Healthcare shows the most consistent growth
  • Growing sectors: Technology, healthcare, green energy, education
  • Declining sectors: Traditional retail, print media

Salary Information

  • Average salary across all sectors: ~3,700 EUR/month (gross)
  • Entry-level positions: 1,800 - 2,500 EUR/month
  • Mid-level positions: 2,500 - 4,500 EUR/month
  • Senior-level positions: 4,500+ EUR/month
  • Berlin average salary: Slightly above German national average

Requirements & Work Models

  • German proficiency required for 68% of positions
  • English-only acceptable for 32% (primarily in tech and international companies)
  • Bachelor's degree or equivalent vocational training required for most positions
  • 38% of new job listings offer remote or hybrid options
  • 2-5 years of experience typically required for mid-level positions
