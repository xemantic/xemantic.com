My name is Kazik Pogoda and I work as creative technologist. A fancy term to say that instead of using Adobe products I write my own software. Expressing myself the way I want to requires writing a custom software. The practice is called creative coding, and the outcome is called generative art or design. I don't feel like an artist though. I majored in philosophy and what I create is rather reflecting my philosophical believes on the evolution applied not only to the context of biology, but also human and machine cultures.

I love to collaborate. If you feel like achieving some of my generative aesthetics in your work, feel free to contact me. Also
